a dolphin (imported model)
a terrain plane with height mapping (solid color)

The dolphin should follow the terrain as it moves.

press:
  gamepad B to move the dolphin forward
  left joystick to turn the dolphin right
  right joystick to orbit avatar
  EQU (=) to toggle window size.
  ESC to abort.

Try experimenting with different terrain, heightmaps, textures, etc.
